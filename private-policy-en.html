Privacy Policy

La Citadelle - Vote Companion

Last updated: 7 December 2025

The La Citadelle – Vote Companion application respects your privacy and does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

1. Data Collection

The application does not collect any personal data such as names, email addresses, IP addresses, device identifiers, account information, or analytics data. No login or registration is required to use the app.

2. Local Storage

The app stores a small amount of information locally on your device, including:

  • Vote cooldown timers
  • Elytreum orb values
  • Rankup checklist progress
  • Notification preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

3. External Websites

The app provides quick access to external voting websites via in-app browser tabs. These third-party websites may use cookies, analytics, or tracking technologies according to their own privacy policies. The app does not control or process data collected by these external websites.

4. Notifications

The app may send local notifications to remind you when voting becomes available again. These notifications do not use or require personal data.

5. Security

Because no personal information is collected or transmitted, the app presents no risk of data exposure or unauthorized access.

6. Children’s Privacy

The application does not collect personal information and may be safely used by users of all ages, including minors.

7. Updates to this Policy

This privacy policy may be updated if necessary. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
